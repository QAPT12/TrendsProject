Welcome!
This site aims to give players a spot to ask questions about the game Magic: the Gathering.

Ask questions about weird edge cases or rulings, or even the simple things that you want to check in on. Users can then respond to posts with the answer to the question, and using community voting, can filter out any incorrect or bad responses.

Got a question? Click the button below to make a post!

and most importantly, Have fun and be nice!
